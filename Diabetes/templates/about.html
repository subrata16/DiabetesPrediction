Our Project

Diabetes Prediction System Using Machine Learning

Our project, the Diabetes Prediction System, utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to predict the likelihood of diabetes in patients based on various health metrics and historical data. By harnessing the power of data science, we aim to provide a tool that can assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions, ultimately contributing to better health management and preventive care.
